Robert Craven

I am a research associate in the Department of Computing at Imperial College London, working on argumentation theory, logics of action, deontic logic and policy analysis. In addition, I'm a research student in the Department of Philosophy at Birkbeck College, University of London, where I work mostly in metaphysics and the philosophy of action, but also in the philosophy of mind, logic, language and ethics. I was an undgraduate in philosophy at King's College, Cambridge, and have an M.Sc. and Ph.D. from Imperial College London in logic and artificial intelligence.
