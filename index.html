Robert Craven

About

I work mostly in metaphysics and the philosophy of action, but also in the philosophy of mind, logic and language. Philosophers whose work interests me include Aristotle, Kant, Frege, Anscombe, McDowell and Rödl.

Previously, I was a research associate in artificial intelligence, working on argumentation theory, logics of action, deontic logic and policy analysis.
